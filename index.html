About Us

Skola Tech Academy & IT Services is a premier educational institution based in Madurai, Tamil Nadu. Our mission is to equip learners with cutting-edge technical knowledge and practical skills, enabling them to excel in the dynamic IT industry.

We offer a diverse range of courses tailored to meet industry demands, including:

  • Advanced Software Training in Data Analytics, Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Robotics.
  • Design and Development programs such as UI/UX Design and Full Stack Web Development (MERN).
  • Software and CAD Certifications in Python, Java, C/C++, AutoCAD, SolidWorks, and Fusion 360.
  • Specialized Programs for TallyPrime, Excel, and Office Automation to enhance job-ready skills.

Key Features

  • Industry-Experienced Trainers: Learn from professionals with real-world experience.
  • Practical Learning: Hands-on training with live projects and industry-standard tools.
  • Flexible Timings: Classes available on weekdays and weekends to fit your schedule.
  • Certifications: Earn completion certificates that boost your career prospects.
  • Placement Support: Assistance with internships and job placements in reputed organizations.

Our Facilities

  • Well-equipped computer labs with high-speed internet.
